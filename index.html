About Us

Who We Are ?

Justice 48 aims to create a united front that is actively taking legal action worldwide to bring the perpetrators of the Palestinian genocide – and those who provide diplomatic and material support to perpetrators – to justice. We provide a resource hub to (a) raise legal awareness within the pro-Palestine solidarity movement, (b) demystifies the law and legal mechanisms and (c) share news and updates on existing legal efforts, to empower grassroots to engage meaningfully with the legal fight.

A United Front

Justice 48 is a united front taking legal action worldwide to hold perpetrators of the Palestinian genocide and their supporters accountable. It serves as a resource hub for those ready to use universal jurisdiction laws, international law, and applicable laws in their own countries to seek justice. 4o

Empowering Grassroots

Our campaign aims to raise awareness about the ongoing genocide of Palestinians and empower grassroots individuals to pursue legal actions for Palestine. We seek to build a global coalition fighting for justice, emphasizing that Palestinians have waited too long for justice. 4o

Our mission

Justice 48 aims to create a united front taking global legal action against perpetrators of the Palestinian genocide and their supporters. We provide resources to raise legal awareness, demystify legal mechanisms, and share updates on legal efforts, empowering grassroots engagement in the legal fight for justice.

Justice Tracker

The Justice 48 Tracker compiles lawsuits filed by legal bodies, organizations, and/or individuals that seek to hold the state of “Israel” and their officials or representatives accountable. It also includes cases that have been filed against other governments, corporations and individuals that are complicit in the “Israeli” occupation and war crimes.
